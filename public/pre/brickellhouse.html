Brickell House

Condo Details

Located on the prestigious Brickell Bay Drive, the BrickellHouse gives residents one of the best locations within this popular area of Miami. The Brickell area, also known as the Financial District, has become one of the most popular areas in Miami for work and to call home. Full of entertainment venues, fine dining and plenty of shops, it's recognized as a high-end destination. The BrickellHouse is a prime Miami location with landmark architecture and many pedestrian-friendly areas. The building features a glass-enclosed corner cafe/bar with a reflecting pool. It also features a triple-height lobby accented with stainless steel, wood and stone finishes. Security includes a 24-hour concierge and access control. With green-building technology, residents get a secure building with an automated parking garage, high-speed wireless access in all common areas and more. The BrickellHouse makes it easy to get from one floor to another with 5 high-speed elevators, as well. Owners gain access to a private event bar including a catering kitchen. There's also a high-definition screening room with theater style seating and a 10-foot screen.
